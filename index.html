About Us

Busega preparatory school is a private,Mixed Day and Boarding primary School, Christian founded in 1989 with 15 students and offers an ideal learning in urban setting. Busega Preparatory is now an advanced school with a farm for project work, computer laboratory for Technology ,advanced learning by quality staff members and fully registerede with the MoES with Reg.no. PPS/B/44 and centre no.003379 at the edge of kampala the capital of Uganda

Our Mission, Vision, and Values

Mission

To excel in imparting practical knowledge and Godly values to the primary leavers,which will help them to function effectively.

Vision

To be the leading provider of life skills and a solid academic performance in Kampala district.

Values

Excellence, Creativity, Teamwork, Literacy and Confidence
